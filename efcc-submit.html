Frequently Asked Questions

What types of crimes can I report?
You can report economic and financial crimes including fraud, money laundering, cybercrime, advance fee fraud, contract scams, and other financial offenses.
Can I report anonymously?
Yes, you can submit a petition anonymously. However, providing your contact information can help us follow up if we need additional details.
What happens after I submit a petition?
Your petition will be reviewed by our team. If it falls within our jurisdiction, an investigation will be initiated. You will be contacted if additional information is needed.
