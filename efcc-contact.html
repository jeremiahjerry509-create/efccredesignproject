Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a petition to the EFCC?
You can submit a petition online through our website, via email at info@efcc.gov.ng, or visit any EFCC office to submit a physical petition.
What information do I need to provide when reporting a crime?
You should provide as much detail as possible including names, dates, locations, amounts involved, and any supporting documents or evidence.
Is my identity protected when I report a crime?
Yes, all petitions are treated with confidentiality. Your identity will be protected unless required by law or with your consent.
How long does it take for EFCC to investigate a case?
Investigation time varies depending on the complexity of the case. We strive to investigate all cases thoroughly and efficiently.
